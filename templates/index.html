Important People

Lauren JuratovacMaid of Honor

Lauren and Brit first met in the second grade when then both firmly declared their dislike for each other due Brit "stealing" Lauren's best friend. They soon put that all behind them and decided it was much easier to cut out the middle-man and become best friends themselves. They have been inseparable for the last 20 years and did EVERYTHING together growing up. This included their (terrible) baseball team, their stint at dance lessons, working at the ski lodge and even living together after high school. Even though Lauren now lives in NYC she is still Brit's closest friend. Brit couldn't imagine a better BFF and considers herself extremely lucky to have Lauren by her side on her wedding day.

Curtiss ClarkBest Man

Rob's okayest brother, they first met when Rob was born. What started as a sibling rivalry became a friendship and brotherhood with age. They have spent meny years together skiing, kayaking, and annoying their parents along the way.

Katie ZbydniewskiMaid of Honor

Katie is basically Brittany's sister. Katie has been a part of Brit's family since she started dating her brother, Drew, a million years ago. Katie is the "yin" to Brit's "yang" and helps balance Brit out during stressful times, like the time Katie went into labor during a family Thanksgiving trip and Brit was convinced she would "HAVE TO DELIVER THAT BABY" in the car on the way to the hospital. Brittany is grateful for the sisterly bond they share, the wonderful niece and nephew she brought into this world and for taking good care of her favorite brother.

Drew ShawGroomsman

Drew is the brother of the Bride. In their short time as friends Rob and Drew have destroyed many golf balls, wrestled with bears, and gambled casinos into debt.

Jessie KonopinskiBridesmaid

Jessie and Brit met after they both joined the exclusive club Team Puerto Rican. Jessie and Brit have more funny stories, awkward adventures and ridiculous memories than either of them can count. Like the time they decided drive to Pittsburg to dance the night away or when Jessie insisted on serenading Brit by playing Hakuna Matata on her trumpet. When they are together it's always full of inside jokes and singing to the crappy pop punk bands they used to love. Brit considers Jessie the true definition of a best friend and can't wait to make new memories with her as their friendship continues to grow.

Nick PavlakGroomsman

Nick and Rob have been friends for nearly 10 years. They became friends early on in college, eventually lived together, and later became business partners. While they no longer run Babl they still work very closely together building websites at their current job.

Teri KrawczkyBridesmaid

Teri and Brit were friends at first sight. They met while working at the same job together and were caught wearing the same shirt at a company meeting. They weren't even allowed to be on the same team during company party games because it "wasn't fair" because they would always win. They have had a special bond during the time they have known each other and have both said that they feel like they've known each other forever.

Greg RyanGroomsman

Greg and Rob have been friends for quite some time. They met when Rob was a freshman in college, and joined the same fraternity where they quaffed many steins. Greg eventually became President of the fraternity and Rob stepped up to become Vice President. They worked so well together during this time that the following year they switched roles.

Jen MarkovichBridesmaid

Brittany and Jen met at work and became good friends over their shared amusement of cats in business suits, face swapping co-workers and a good cocktail. Brittany and Jen text each other daily, maybe even hourly, because they have the exact same sense of humor. They can be found at social gathering giggling together over fart jokes, singing to Paul Simon or talking about animals. Brittany cherishes her friendship with Jen and her beautiful big heart.

Greg PasicznykGroomsman

Greg P, the other half of Greg Squared is an old college friend of Rob. He was one of the first to get Rob (and many others) into craft beer drinking. At one point while Greg was working for Brooklyn Brewing, he lived on Rob's couch in exchange for nightly tastings.

Jessica CuglewskiBridesmaid

Brittany and Jessica went to high school together but it was until they moved to Cleveland Heights did they truly become best friends. Brittany credits Jessica for helping her realize that she shouldn't settle for anything less than the best in life. Together, they can turn any serious situation into a laugh-till-you-pee-your-pants kind of night where everyone gets into a little bit of trouble but it was totally worth it.

Adam SilverGroomsman
Arlee RustBridesmaid

Arlee is more than just a bridesmaid, she is the "work wife" of the bride. While, Brittany and Arlee are technically just former co-workers who became best friends....they will always be "work wives". They share a huge bond because they both work in Animal Welfare and dream of a day when they open up a cat sanctuary, on the beach somewhere...with unicorns and rainbows. At social gatherings you can find them in the corner staring at each other's phones sharing pictures of their pets. Brittany credits Arlee with helping her stay strong during emotional times at work and she will always be forever grateful for the bond they share.

Jason BoryczkaGroomsman
